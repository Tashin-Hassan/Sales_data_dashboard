**Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)**
**Project Overview**

This project presents a professional business intelligence dashboard developed using Microsoft Power BI to analyze sales performance and support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful insights across multiple dimensions, including time, geography, customer segments, and product categories.

**Objectives**

The primary objective of this project is to deliver a comprehensive analytical solution for monitoring and evaluating business performance.

**Key goals include:**

Tracking overall sales, profit, and operational performance
Identifying high-performing regions and product categories
Analyzing customer segment contributions
Understanding sales trends over time
Evaluating the relationship between discount strategies and profitability

The dashboard includes multiple visualizations to support in-depth analysis.

Regional sales comparison
Product sub-category performance breakdown
Geographic distribution of sales using map visualization

**Key Insights**

The analysis reveals several important business insights.

The West region contributes the highest share of total sales
Product categories such as Phones and Chairs are major revenue drivers
Higher discount levels do not necessarily lead to increased profitability
Sales trends indicate variability, suggesting possible seasonal patterns
